About PR-S Music

PR-S Music is a music streaming service developed by Robipiot Inc. Users select music to stream to their device on-demand, or they can listen to existing playlists. The service also includes the Internet radio station PR-S Music 1, PRS Music Hits, and PRS Music, which broadcast live to over 20 countries 24 hours a day.

With over 75 million songs, PRS Music offers a vast library of content for music lovers of all genres. Whether you're into pop, rock, hip-hop, classical, or anything in between, you'll find something to love on PRS Music.
